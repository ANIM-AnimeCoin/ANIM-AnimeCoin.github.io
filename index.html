We aim—first and foremost—to be the fairest, most transparent, secure, decentralized, and honest mastercoin on the crypto market.

We will do this while specifically appealing to the anime community with exciting features, creating an immediate use-case for the new ANIM coins.

We will leverage the ability to issue "provably rare" anime-related digital images on the blockchain that can then traded on a decentralized exchange for ANIM coins.
