We aim — first and foremost — to be the most fair, transparent, secure, decentralized, and honest mastercoin on the crypto market.

We will do this while specifically appealing to the anime community with exciting features, creating an immediate use-case for the new ANIME coins.

This use case is the ability to issue "rare" digital art on the blockchain that can then be traded on a decentralized exchange for the new ANIME coins.
